What is The Coders?

Welcome to The Coders, the hub for computer science and technology enthusiasts at Queens College, The City University of New York. We're a diverse and inclusive community, welcoming students at all skill levels. From coding workshops to hackathons, our club provides opportunities for skill development and networking. Join us to unlock your potential and be a part of the dynamic world of technology right here at Queens College.
